Moderation workflow

  1. Open the queue and filter by pending, approved, merged, or rejected.
  2. Search by name, email, city, quote, or item code.
  3. Open a submission, save notes, then approve, merge, or reject.
  4. Use owner search to avoid duplicate profiles before approval.
  5. Use bulk reject for spam or duplicate batches.
